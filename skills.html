Skills

  1. You need to put in as much practice as you can. Take occasional rests to rest your eyes, but keep practicing as your main priority.

  2. Understand and master the game's mechanics. To get accustomed to the gameplay, experiment with all of the options.

  3. Find a playstyle. How would you like to play? Do you intend to be combative? Defensive? Perhaps you want a fighter, a tank, a support, or perhaps two fighters!

  4. Have a backup playstyle at all times. You want a secondary function like a tank or support if your primary duty is a fighter. Keep in mind that sometimes you can't get what you want, especially in games.

  5. Find out how your opponent plays. Knowing how a team plays and understanding how to play against them is crucial if you ever find yourself competing against them.
